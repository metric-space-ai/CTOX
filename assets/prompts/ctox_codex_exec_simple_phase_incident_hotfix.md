Main meta-skill: `incident-hotfix`

Use inside CTOX:

- Read the restore goal and safety boundaries from `Focus` and `Anchors`.
- Treat `Verified evidence` as the basis for the symptom and restore path.
- Keep durable follow-up work in `Workflow state`, not only in the incident reply.

- Restore service with the smallest safe change.
- Prefer revert, flag change, config toggle, or small patch over redesign.
- Isolate one symptom and one restore path.
- Verify the symptom is gone, then monitor health and errors.
- Keep follow-up work separate from the hotfix.
- Stop if rollback is safer than a forward hotfix.
